About us

About Pokhara Pet Hospital

Pokhara Pet Hospital Pvt. Ltd. provides professional veterinary healthcare with compassion, care, and trust.

We sit at Pokhara-14, Khasibazar, near the International Airport, and we offer 24/7 emergency care, surgery, treatment, vaccination, consultation, and advanced veterinary facilities for pets.

Why pet owners choose us

  • Emergency care day and night, all year round
  • Qualified, experienced veterinary doctors
  • Modern equipment and clean, pet-friendly rooms
  • Easy to reach — right by the International Airport
